# 🌊 Instagram Specialist Onboarding Summary

## Project Context: Splashdeals
Splashdeals is Serbia's premier water park ticket marketplace. It uses a high-performance **Next.js 16 (PPR)** stack with a custom **Aquastream UI** design system.

## Brand Identity & Design
- **Aesthetic**: "Hydro-Premium" - immersive, vibrant, fluid, premium.
- **Visuals**: Void Navy backgrounds, Electric Cyan primary actions, high-refraction glassmorphism.
- **THE PURPLE BAN**: **NO** violet, indigo, or purple hues. Stick to Aqua, Teal, Slate, and Azure.
- **Typography**: Geist Sans/Mono, chiseled and precise.
- **Motion**: Fluid animations via `framer-motion`.

## Technical Ecosystem
- **Framework**: Next.js 16 (PPR-first).
- **Architecture**: Split into `(web)`, `(mob)`, `(server)`, and `(admin)`.
- **Bridge**: Communicates with OpenClaw agents via `.agent/bridge/`.
- **Data**: Multi-schema Prisma (public, partners, sales).

## Instagram Specialist Role
- **Goal**: Drive engagement and sales via Instagram (Reels, Stories, Feed).
- **Compliance**: Adhere to Aquastream UI (No Purple).
- **Language**: Serbian (Latin) primary.
- **Reporting**: Report to Social Team Lead.

## Next Steps
- Coordinate with Social Team Lead on the first batch of Reels/Stories.
- Audit existing facility media in the database for Instagram potential.
- Ensure Instagram links in `Footer.tsx` are updated when accounts are live.
